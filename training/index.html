Kabuterimon boosts HP-, Defense- and Speed-training
recruit Seadramon and Greymon and go to the building on the right in beetleland - talk to the kabuterimon at the bottom left and select the second option
Trainingmanual boosts all training
recruit Piximon and go to the big shop, Piximon will appear randomly behind the counter and offer you the training manual for 50'000bits
